full screen

The geographical nature of the network is displayed with color coding serving the purpose of links. The groups were generated by Gephi's incomplete Louvain algorithm. More on the difference between Gephi and R community detection in the timline below.
Citation
Algorithm:
Vincent D Blondel, Jean-Loup Guillaume, Renaud Lambiotte, Etienne Lefebvre, Fast unfolding of communities in large networks, in Journal of Statistical Mechanics: Theory and Experiment 2008 (10), P1000
Resolution:
R. Lambiotte, J.-C. Delvenne, M. Barahona Laplacian Dynamics and Multiscale Modular Structure in Networks 2009
Conversion of 2-mode Scholar to Interest network to 1-mode Scholar to Scholar network: Sci2 Team. (2009). Science of Science (Sci2) Tool. Indiana University and SciTech Strategies, https://sci2.cns.iu.edu.

So, who or what is in a modularity class?

After Gephi assigned a group number to each entry, I found that I was mostly terrible at identifying the core meanings of those groups by eye.

Enter R and the stylo package to save the day. I created documents of the interest fields from each group and used the oppose() function to identify key preferred and avoided terms for each group.
